The Gofu Fermentation Ladder

A progression from fresh Gofu to living fermented traditions — start anywhere, go as deep as you like.

Fresh Gofu
Simple bean curd made from many kinds of beans.
↓
Level 1 – Soak Fermentation
Extended soaking begins natural fermentation and improves digestibility.
↓
Level 2 – Lactic Fermented Gofu
Brine fermentation produces tangy, probiotic bean cheeses.
↓
Level 3 – Koji Gofu
Advanced fermentations using koji cultures can produce miso-like pastes and aged bean foods.
