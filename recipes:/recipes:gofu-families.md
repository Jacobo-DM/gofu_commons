# Gofu for Families  
**Public Domain — CC0 1.0**

A simple, nourishing food children can make with parents or guardians.  
Perfect for gifts, holidays, and weekend cooking.

## Ideas

- **Cinnamon Gofu Stars**  
  Press Gofu into a flat slab, cut with star cookie cutters, brush with a little oil, sprinkle cinnamon + sugar, and bake until warm.

- **Lime + Chili Gofu Cubes**  
  Cut firm Gofu into cubes, toss with lime juice, chili, and salt. Serve with fresh vegetables or rice.

- **Sweet Miso–Glazed Gofu**  
  Grill or pan-sear slices of Gofu, then brush with a mixture of miso, a little sweetener, and water.

- **Fruit + Honey Silken Gofu**  
  Blend soft Gofu with fruit and a touch of honey for a simple dessert or breakfast bowl.

Families are invited to write their own variations and share them freely.
