Gofu Commons

Gofu is a simple, nourishing bean-based food offered freely to the world. It belongs to no one — and to everyone.

The Commons Declaration

By this declaration:

  • No individual, organization, company, or government may claim ownership, exclusivity, trademark, copyright, or patent protection over Gofu or its core processes.
  • Communities are encouraged to adapt Gofu using local beans, flavors, and cultural foodways of their region.
  • Gofu may be used in shelters, school cafeterias, mutual aid kitchens, home cooking, regenerative agriculture programs — anywhere nourishment and sovereignty are needed.
  • Teaching Gofu is itself part of the commons: lessons, workshops, Discovery Mode modules, and Monster Kitchen stories may all be freely created and shared.
  • No one is obligated to attribute this declaration, but all are invited to carry its spirit: food as a right, not a commodity; nourishment as relationship, not extraction.
Gofu is offered openly, joyfully, and irreversibly to the world.
May it travel far.
May it feed many.
May its simplicity strengthen the hands that need it most.
